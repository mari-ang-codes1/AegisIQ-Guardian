Core Philosophy: Security is a skill, not a feature. Certification attainment is the baseline for defensive capability.

Primary: AZ-204 (Developing Azure Compute & Storage Solutions — Foundation for secure API dev).

Secondary: AZ-305 (Designing Infrastructure Solutions — Focus on Zero Trust architecture).

DevOps Engineer (Pipeline Security focus):

Primary: AZ-400 (Designing and Implementing DevOps Solutions — Focus on DevSecOps & Pipeline integrity).

2. Readiness Protocols (AegisIQ Thresholds)
To ensure personnel are capable of maintaining our security posture, all certification prep must adhere to the following logic:

Study Cadence: 1–2 hours of focused study (Cognitive load management).

Assessment Rhythm: Weekly checkpoints (Required to detect knowledge decay).

Readiness Gate: Minimum 75% practice score required.

Note: Personnel scoring <75% are flagged as 'High-Risk' for operational errors.

3. Operational Integration (Work IQ Baseline)
Optimal Learning Window: 12–18 weekly meeting hours.

Risk Escalation: Personnel exceeding 20 meeting hours/week are auto-flagged in the SOC dashboard. Cognitive fatigue directly increases the probability of overlooking social engineering indicators.
